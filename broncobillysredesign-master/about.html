Bronco Billy's has been in business since the 80s. It's Western Theme is inspired by American actor, Bronco Billy Anderson. He was the first star to emerge in the Western film Genre.

Bronco Billy's was one of the first pizza parlors to open in the Niles area. Mike along with other owners thought it would be a great business venture. The Niles location opened around 1988. They successfully opened other locations in Union City and Fremont. The Hayward location opened in 1992.
